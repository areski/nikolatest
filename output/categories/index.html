Emergency Dialer
One auto dialer software for infinite needs.

Emergency numbers can be fed into the auto-dialer device so that when triggered during times of emergency, an automatic call is connected to that specific number for assistance. The numbers can be those of your family members, friends, doctors or emergency services like fire or police.

What is Autodialing?

A software that can be used to automatically call thousands of numbers from a phone list or database. 

Once the call has been answered, the autodialer either plays a recorded message or connects the call to a live person.
